Eligibility Criteria for Education Loan

The following criteria generally apply to applicants seeking an education loan:

  • Nationality: Applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • Age Limit: The student should be between 18 to 35 years of age.
  • Academic Qualification: Must have secured admission to a recognised university/institution in India or abroad.
  • Co-Applicant Requirement: A co-applicant such as a parent, spouse, or guardian with a steady income is generally required.
  • Creditworthiness: The credit score and repayment capacity of the co-applicant are considered for loan approval.
  • Courses Covered: Loans are offered for graduation, post-graduation, diploma, and professional courses in various streams.
